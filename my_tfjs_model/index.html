What dog breed is this?

This interactive tool is based on https://github.com/tensorflow/tfjs-examples/tree/master/mobilenet.

To use this tool, simply upload an image of a dog and wait a few seconds for the model to process your image. Beside the image you will see the top three predicted breeds for the dog in your image. The number next to each breed name shows the probability that the dog in your image belongs to that breed.
